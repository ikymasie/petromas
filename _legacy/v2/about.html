Company Overview

A Legacy of Trust in Energy

Petromas is a premier energy solutions provider headquartered in the Fairground Office Park, Gaborone. As a 100% Botswana citizen-owned entity, we take pride in our deep local roots and our expanding Pan-African reach.

Our operations are built on the pillars of transparency, reliability, and strategic growth, enabling us to serve as a preferred partner for both government entities and private sector leaders.

Our Vision

To become the preferred partner for African petroleum logistics, recognized for driving efficiency in every drop and setting the benchmark for ethical operations in the energy sector.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality petroleum products and seamless logistics solutions through innovation, strategic asset acquisition, and a commitment to sustainable development.
